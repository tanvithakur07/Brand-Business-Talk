Campaign Analysis

Entertain first. Deliver next.

01
Nostalgia Marketing — Lockdown Engagement

#90sReDun — Iconic Ads Revival

Lockdown insight: 90s nostalgia trending. Dunzo recreated Surf, Cadbury, FeviKwik ads with modern twist — pure social gold.

Didn't sell delivery — sold joyful memory triggers. Urban millennials shared instantly.

"Nostalgia disarms resistance. #90sReDun made Dunzo memorable without feeling like an ad."

Social-first virality kept brand top-of-mind during consumption surge. Emotional equity established.

8.5
Success Rating Nostalgia-driven social media domination
02
Performance Marketing — Quick Commerce

Dunzo Daily — 19-Min Grocery

Pain point: grocery wait times. Promise: 19 minutes. "Grocery ka drama chhodo" = frustration → solution perfection.

Bollywood dialogues + speed obsession made functional benefit culturally addictive.

"Clear promises convert. Dunzo Daily made 19-min delivery the new grocery expectation."

Full-funnel: video → app → first order. Performance tracking optimized every dollar spent.
