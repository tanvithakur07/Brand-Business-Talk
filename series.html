The 40 Day | 40 Brand Series is BBT's most ambitious editorial campaign — publishing one deeply researched brand case study every single day for 40 consecutive days, focused exclusively on leading Indian brands.

Each case study decodes a brand's marketing campaigns, business strategy, SWOT analysis, growth playbook, and expert insights — converting complex research into actionable intelligence for entrepreneurs, professionals, and marketers.
